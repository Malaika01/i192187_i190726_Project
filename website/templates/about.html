Curatio Morbi at a Glance

Welcome to our website! Curatio Morbi, which translates to Treatment of diseases, is a cryptographic solution based on Elliptic curve cryptography and Diffie-Hellman algorithm, to secure model weights during aggregation. This approach is computationally efficient and does not reduce the overall model accuracy.

Our website provides a platform where hospitals can upload their trained models. Once the aggregated model is ready, hospitals can download it. The models are aggregated by the server. We assure the security of the shared model through our highly sophisticated encryption algorithms.

We aim to reduce the patient's data sharing concerns of hospitals!
